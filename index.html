How the pipeline works

Core workflow

  • ✓Dataset selection: Use a safe, anonymized dataset from a trusted source such as Kaggle.
  • ✓Feature preparation: Convert raw inputs into model-ready numerical features.
  • ✓Model training: Compare a few classifiers and choose the one that generalizes best.
  • ✓Evaluation: Check recall and confusion matrix, not only accuracy.
